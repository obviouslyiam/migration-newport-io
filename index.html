About

A Family Office Built Around Technology

Newport Capital LLC is a private family office focused on technology investments, digital property development, and strategic business operations.

We build and hold a concentrated portfolio of software platforms, data products, and digital media properties — operating them for the long term rather than optimizing for exits.

Our approach is operator-led: hands-on with product, engineering, and growth across every holding in the portfolio.
